Saldana's Tavern

Saldanas Tavern was established in 20XX by Kevin Saldana. Determined to establish location with a rustic and classy atmosphere to his home town reminiscent of his home island of 'Sombra Moses', he set out on a journey to form what would later become one of the most recognized venues in the area.
